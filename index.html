Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Jason Tella-in, an Electronics Engineer by profession and a passionate hobbyist photographer. I specialize in data analysis, troubleshooting, root cause analysis, circuit analysis, and performance validation, applying my technical skills to deliver practical and innovative solutions. Over the years, I’ve worked on various projects that have helped me grow and refine my expertise in real-world scenarios.

While photography is my creative outlet and a way to unwind, it also allows me to view the world through fresh perspectives. Whether capturing landscapes, urban exploration, portraits, or special moments, photography provides a unique form of expression and inspiration, balancing the structured nature of my engineering work.

My Journey So Far

After passing the board exam and becoming a licensed Electronics Engineer, I spent three years as a Failure Analysis Engineer at Analog Devices. In this role, I evaluated devices to assess their functionality, identified damage mechanisms, and distinguished between functional and defective units. I provided clear technical assessments to support product reliability and quality, gaining hands-on experience with advanced failure analysis tools such as Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), Emission Microscopy, ELITE and many others. Working closely with cross-functional teams, I turned detailed failure data into actionable insights that helped improve processes and products.

Alongside my engineering career, I have always had a keen interest in photography, which I’ve developed since my 4th year in college. While photography is a creative outlet rather than a career, I decided to leverage this interest when I chose to build this website. I intentionally created it as a way to explore software development, improve my technical skills, and showcase my photography work as one of the projects.

This website project allowed me to dive into web development, learn new technologies, and solve complex problems with the help of AI technology. Leveraging AI tools enhanced both the development process and the functionality of the site. This experience deepened my interest in the software field, and I’m excited to continue learning and contributing, with the goal of joining a dynamic team in the tech industry.
